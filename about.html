ABOUT LIHAM CAFE

Welcome to Liham Cafe, your cozy spot in the heart of Vinzons, Camarines Norte! At Liham, we're all about bringing you fantastic coffee and a one-of-a-kind experience.

Well, besides our awesome espresso and milk-based drinks, we've got a soft spot for nostalgia and connection. Ever thought about writing a letter to your future self or a loved one? We've got you covered. Scribble down your thoughts, and we'll make sure it gets to the right place - whether that's your doorstep or our cafe after a year.

Liham Cafe isn't just about great coffee; it's about creating memories and preserving messages. Swing by for a sip, a smile, and maybe a letter that'll make you grin a year from now. Cheers to coffee and connection at Liham Cafe!

WHAT IS LIHAM PARA SA HINAHARAP

"Liham para sa hinaharap" is a unique concept of Liham Cafe that allows its customers to write a letter to their future selves or to anyone else. These letters, sealed with wax, are then stored in envelope boxes at Liham Cafe and can be picked up or mailed to the provided address on the desired date next year. Let's say you write a letter on 14 February 2024, your letter can be picked up or mailed on or before 31 December 2025.

WHAT TO WRITE?

Your current situation. Kumusta ka? Anong pinagkakaabalahan mo? Isulat mo lahat ng gustong mong isulat na nararamdaman mo o naiisip mo ngayon. Let the words flow. Breathe as you open up and share your feelings.

Things you are grateful for. Anong mga bagay o event o tao sa buhay mo na gusto mong pasalamatan? Ito ay makakatulong upang mas mag-focus ka sa blessings na iyong natanggap.

Long-term goals. Anong goal na gusto mong tuparin sa loob ng mahigit isang taon? Gusto mong maging CPA, Chef, Doctor, Lawyer, Architect, Seaman? You can turn your dreams into reality as long as you set your mind to it.

Short-term goals. Anong goal mo na gusto mong tuparin sa loob ng isang taon para ma-achieve mo ang long- term goals mo? Take it one step at a time.

STEPS GUIDES FOR

Liham para sa Hinaharap

STEP 1
Buy a Liham Set (includes postcards, wax seal beads, and envelope).
STEP 2
Write anything on the postcards which can be addressed to yourself, a family member, a relative, or anyone else.
STEP 3
Insert postcards into the envelope and seal it in the sealing station.
STEP 4
Write the following on the envelope: Back: Name and complete address; Front: Desired mailing or pick up date.
STEP 5
Fill out the registration slip with your contact details. Put the envelope in the box with the desired mailing or pick up date next year.

And you are done! You can come back to Liham Cafe to pick up your letter or have it mailed to the address provided.
